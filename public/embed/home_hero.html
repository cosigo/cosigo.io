COSIGO

A governance-first system for representing registered silver on-chain.

Anchor: 1 milligram of registered silver equals 1 Cosigo token.

  • Physical foundation: token supply is constrained by registered metal.
  • Transparent verification: on-chain records plus documented evidence.
  • Optional participation: satellites may remain inactive indefinitely.
References: Satellites · Source · Contracts

This overview is descriptive. It does not promise returns or require participation.
